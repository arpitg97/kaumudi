About us

Incorporated in 2013, Kaumudi Textile Digital Print & Design Studio is located in Jaipur, Rajasthan, India. Providing digital printing services on all fabrics (Polyester, Cotton, Viscose, Flex, Silk, Modal, Lycra, Rayon, Linen, Dari, Casement, Duck and many more).

Designing is the core process of our company. We have a powerful team of experienced designers creating exclusive and unique designs for our clients. Customers satisfaction is what keeps us going. We also have certified 24/7 in-house engineers, ensuring smooth operations.

Design is the most significant aspect of print. Design is what adds life and beauty to any print. Textile Digital Print

With our years of experience in textile digital printing, we bring quality digital printed fabric. Digital printing is provided on different types of fabrics like, all kind of polyester, cotton, viscose, silk, nylon, etc. The patterns for printing can be exclusive and unique with multicolor designs. Lot of accuracy and precision is used during printing to make even the smallest print unique. A state-of-the-art technology, combined with skilled manpower, gives the ultimate best result on the fabric.
